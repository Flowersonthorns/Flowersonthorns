Who We Are

Flowers On Thorns is a community-based organization located in Juakali Marurui slums, Roysambu Sub-County. Since 2015, we have supported vulnerable youth and young mothers with opportunities for healing, growth, and empowerment.

Psychosocial Support

Helping individuals heal and build resilience through group and individual therapy.

Skills Training

We offer vocational training in catering, hairdressing & beauty, tailoring, and parenting.

Life Skills Education

Equipping our beneficiaries with knowledge and tools to navigate daily life and decision-making.
