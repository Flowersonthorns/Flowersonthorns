Programs & Impact

Flowers On Thorns offers a wide range of support services and vocational training for youth and young mothers in Roysambu. Our holistic model ensures that beneficiaries not only gain skills but also receive psychosocial support, mentorship, and reintegration assistance.

1.Rescue Support for Young Mothers

Temporary shelter, food, pads, diapers, trauma healing.

2.Psycho-Social Rehabilitation for Street Youth.

o Counseling, mentorship, reintegration.

3.Vocational Skills Training

o Hairdressing, beauty, catering,business skills.

4.Youth Economic Empowerment Projects

Trolley vending project for income generation.

5.SHOFCO Youth Empowerment Partnership

o GBV, mental health, beadwork, yoghurt making, and savings training.

6.Planned Initiative

Rescue Home for Young Mothers & Girls.

Safe shelter, psychosocial care, and rehabilitation.
