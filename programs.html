Our Programs & Impact

We run targeted programs to equip youth and young mothers with skills, confidence, and opportunities to create sustainable livelihoods and brighter futures.

Hairdressing & Beauty Training

We offer professional training in hairdressing and beauty, enabling participants to start their own businesses or secure employment in salons.

Catering & Hospitality

Our catering program teaches participants how to prepare, present, and manage food services, creating opportunities in the food and hospitality sector.

Tailoring & Fashion Design

This program trains participants in garment making and fashion design, equipping them with skills to start tailoring businesses or join the garment industry.

Psychosocial Support

We provide counseling, group therapy, and mentorship to promote mental health and personal growth among vulnerable individuals.

Impact Highlights

  • Over 200 youth and mothers trained since inception
  • Increased household income and food security
  • Improved mental well-being among participants
  • Community engagement and stronger support networks
