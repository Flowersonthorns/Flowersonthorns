Programs & Impact

Flowers On Thorns offers a wide range of support services and vocational training for youth and young mothers in Roysambu. Our holistic model ensures that beneficiaries not only gain skills but also receive psychosocial support, mentorship, and reintegration assistance.

1. Hairdressing and Beauty

Training in hair care, styling, braiding, and beauty therapy to equip young women with marketable skills. Graduates often begin their own salons or join established beauty businesses.

2. Catering and Baking

Our kitchen-based program empowers youth with practical cooking, baking, and customer service skills. Some of our alumni have started small eateries and baking businesses.

3. Tailoring and Fashion Design

Teaching basic tailoring, pattern making, and fabric design. This program is especially popular among young mothers aiming for flexible home-based work.

4. Therapy and Counseling

We provide individual and group therapy, especially for youth facing trauma, depression, or abuse. The safe space created fosters healing and hope.

5. Outreach & Community Engagement

Flowers On Thorns organizes community sensitization forums, school visits, and parent engagement sessions that promote youth welfare and family support.

6. Economic Empowerment

We help graduates form savings groups, link them to internship opportunities, and provide startup kits to kickstart their entrepreneurial journeys.
