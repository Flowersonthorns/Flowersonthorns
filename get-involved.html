Get Involved

Flowers On Thorns thrives through community support and partnerships. Whether you're an individual, an organization, or a group, there are many ways to join us in transforming lives:

1. Volunteer

We welcome professionals and skilled individuals who are passionate about youth empowerment. You can volunteer as a trainer, mentor, counselor, or mobilizer. Both short-term and long-term opportunities are available.

2. Partner With Us

We collaborate with training institutions, churches, local businesses, CBOs, and other well-wishers. Our partners contribute through mentorship, sponsorship, technical support, or facilities.

3. Donate

Your financial support helps us provide materials, startup kits, meals, transport stipends, and care packages for our beneficiaries. We are currently fundraising for a training center and outreach events.

4. Spread the Word

Help us reach more people by sharing our story, attending our events, or inviting us to your forums, churches, and social platforms.

5. Offer Internships

Institutions and organizations can support youth reintegration by offering internship slots to our trainees after graduation. This practical exposure builds confidence and employability.

6. Provide Supplies

We accept donations of care packages such as food stuffs,Pads,diapers,underwears and supplies including startup kits, clothing, toiletries, school items, and stationery.

Contact Us
