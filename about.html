About Us

Our Journey

Flowers On Thorns started in 2015 as Jua Kali Young Moms Self-Help Group in the Juakali Marurui Slums of Roysambu. In 2019, we rebranded to Flowers On Thorns CBO.

Our Vision

A thriving community with productive, independent, and transformed youth.

Our Mission

To build a sustainable future for the youth through psychosocial support, education, and life skills training.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy: We walk with our youth, not ahead of them.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, trust, and accountability.
  • Transformation: Every thorn can become a flower.
  • Partnership: Together we grow stronger communities.

Our Team

Ruth Njeri

Flowers On Thorns CBO was founded by Ruth Njeri, a 39-year-old community mobilizer who was born and raised in Juakali Marurui slums in Roysambu Sub-County, Nairobi. Ruth’s life was marked by early struggles, including dropping out of school due to a lack of sanitary pads, falling into addiction, and becoming involved in criminal activities. Her journey took a dark turn with time spent in prison and the tragic loss of many friends and family members to violence, disease, and imprisonment. Despite these adversities, Ruth’s story of resilience and redemption inspired her to create Flowers On Thorns CBO. She founded the organization to support young people, particularly those facing similar challenges, and provide them with opportunities for change. Programs and Services.

Led by founder Ruth Njeri and a passionate team of volunteers, social workers, trainers, and local partners, Flowers On Thorns remains rooted in community and driven by love.
