About Us

Who We Are

Flowers On Thorns is a community-based organization in Roysambu, Nairobi, dedicated to empowering vulnerable youth and young mothers. We provide psychosocial support, vocational skills training, and life skills education to help individuals achieve self-reliance and lead dignified lives.

Our History

Founded in 2015 as the Jua Kali Young Moms Self-Help Group, our organization rebranded to Flowers On Thorns in 2020 to reflect our commitment to helping individuals bloom despite challenges. We were officially registered as a CBO on November 19, 2024.

Our Mission

To empower and transform the lives of vulnerable youth and young mothers through holistic support, skills training, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A community where vulnerable individuals have equal opportunities to thrive, achieve economic independence, and live fulfilling lives.
