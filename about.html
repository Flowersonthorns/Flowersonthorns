Who We Are

Flowers On Thorns CBO is a registered community-based organization located in Juakali Marurui Slums, Roysambu Sub-county, Nairobi County, Kenya. The CBO was born out of a desire to restore hope, dignity, and transformation to vulnerable youth and young mothers affected by poverty, trauma, drug abuse, and gender-based violence.

Originally founded in 2015 as the Jua Kali Young Moms Self-Help Group, the group rebranded in 2019 and was officially registered as a CBO on 19th November 2024 under the Ministry of Labour and Social Protection.

Our Vision

To see a community where youth and young mothers are healed, empowered, and transformed to live meaningful and independent lives.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable youth and young mothers through psychosocial support, life skills education, vocational training, and economic empowerment programs.

Our Objectives

  • To offer rehabilitation and psychosocial support services to youth affected by addiction, trauma, and violence.
  • To equip youth and young mothers with vocational and life skills for self-reliance.
  • To promote gender equality and create awareness on gender-based violence.
  • To build strategic partnerships with stakeholders for sustainable impact.

Our Approach

We take a holistic approach rooted in community and lived experience. Many of our volunteers and leaders are survivors and empowered beneficiaries who give back by mentoring and training others. We provide safe spaces, counseling, training, and tools to help youth heal and thrive.
