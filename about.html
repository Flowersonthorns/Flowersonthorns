Our Story

Flowers On Thorns CBO is a community-driven initiative based in Roysambu, Nairobi County, Kenya. It was founded by Ruth Njeri, a 39-year-old community mobilizer, whose life journey through hardship, addiction, imprisonment, and loss inspired her to give back by empowering vulnerable youth. Raised in Juakali Marurui slums, Ruth overcame early struggles and founded the organization to provide hope and support to youth facing similar challenges.

The organization began in 2015 as Jua Kali Young Moms Self-Help Group and rebranded to Flowers On Thorns in 2020, officially registering as a CBO in November 2024. The name reflects the beauty and strength that can emerge from adversity.

Mission

Building a sustainable future for the youth through psychosocial support, education, and life skills training.

Vision

A thriving community with productive, independent, and transformed youth.

Leadership

  • Ruth Njeri – Chairperson & Founder
  • Priscilla Chavoka – Secretary & Co-founder
  • Pst. Douglas Gituri – Treasurer & Trustee

The team is supported by 5–10 volunteers and professionals who serve as trainers, mentors, and advisors.

Our Approach

We focus on holistic support through psychosocial care, vocational training, life skills, and income-generating projects. Our work is rooted in lived experience, empathy, and community-driven action.
