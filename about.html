About Us

Our Journey

Flowers On Thorns started in 2015 as Jua Kali Young Moms Self-Help Group in the Juakali Marurui Slums of Roysambu. In 2020, we rebranded to Flowers On Thorns CBO and became officially registered on 19th November 2024.

Our Mission

To empower vulnerable youth and young mothers with psychosocial support, skills training, and personal transformation that enables reintegration and independence.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy: We walk with our youth, not ahead of them.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, trust, and accountability.
  • Transformation: Every thorn can become a flower.
  • Partnership: Together we grow stronger communities.

Our Team

Led by founder Ruth Njeri and a passionate team of volunteers, social workers, trainers, and local partners, Flowers On Thorns remains rooted in community and driven by love.
